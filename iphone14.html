iStore trade-in
Start the process from the comfort of your own home - we’ll collect your previously loved iPhone, evaluate it and send you an iStore gift card to the value of your trade-in.
Steps for online trade-in
Step 1: iPhone valuation
Start by getting an estimated trade in value through our online calculator, click here.
Step 2: Online trade in service
Complete our newly updated online trade in collection form.
Step 3: Get your iPhone ready for collection
Prepare your unit for collection, turn off Find My iPhone, back up your device.
Step 4: Courier collection
A courier will be sent to collect your unit within 3 working days.
Step 5: Trade-in Centre diagnostics
Following a hands on diagnostics assessment at our Trade-In Centre, you’ll be offered a final value to accept.
Step 6: Final Trade-in
An iStore Gift card will be issued within 3 working days of accepting your Final Trade-In value. If you make use of an online trade in kit, you can get a credit back on the card used for the online purchase.
*Please ensure you do a full back-up of your iPhone before courier collection. If Find my iPhone is not deactivated, we will not be able to book an appointment.

What capacity is right for me?

64GB
For those who make use of cloud services and stream your content rather than download for offline use. You don’t have a lot of photos and don’t store too many games or apps on your iPhone.
128GB
For those who use a mix of cloud services and the iPhone’s local storage for downloaded content. Offers good space for photos, music and apps.
256GB
For those who want most of their content downloaded for offline use. You’ll have enough space for music, photos, videos and apps.
512GB
For those who don’t want to worry about storage and want access to content for offline use. Great for those who travel often. You will have plenty of storage space for photos, videos, music and more.

Free iCare Plus for iPhone


You can qualify for Free iCare Plus for iPhone when you opt-in for product and service updates as well as WhatsApp communication from iStore. After you've purchased your new iPhone, you can simply sign up for Free iCare Plus for iPhone by filling in the form sent via email. You would need to register within 14 days of purchasing your new iPhone.


The cover will be successfully activated once you have opted in to the relevant marketing communication and registered your iPhone(s). Once you've opted in, we'll confirm the activation of your Free iCare Plus for iPhone. You should also keep this email on hand in the event you need to make any claims. Should you decide to opt-out of iStore product and service updates and WhatsApp communication at any point during the 24 months of Free iCare Plus for iPhone coverage, your Free iCare Plus for iPhone will be deactivated and your free cover will no longer be valid.


If you'd prefer to not opt-in but would still like to buy iCare Plus please contact us on one of our support channels.

How does it work?
iStore and FNB now give you the option of buying your Apple product with a reduced interest rate payment plan. After 12 or 24 months you’ll have the choice to trade in your product, which will offset your outstanding instalments and allow you to purchase a new product. Or you can continue to pay the monthly instalment until the end of the contract term.
Here's how it works:
Select your qualifying Apple product and head to your shopping cart.
Select FNB Budget Option in the payment section at checkout.
Select the payment term and input your ID number. Continue to pay using a qualifying FNB credit card.
Full amount of your purchase comes off your straight facility.
Purchase amount transferred on budget facilty within 3-5 working days.
11.75% interest rate is applied on monthly instalments.
